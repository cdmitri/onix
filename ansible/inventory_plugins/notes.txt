plugin based on:
https://github.com/ansible/ansible/blob/stable-2.7/lib/ansible/plugins/inventory/tower.py
https://docs.ansible.com/ansible/latest/plugins/inventory/tower.html

plugin to be deployed under:
 ~/.ansible/plugins/inventory folder.

    ansible-inventory -i onix_inventory.yml --graph -vvv

check vars with:
    ansible-config dump
